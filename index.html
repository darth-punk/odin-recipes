Description of Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish of wide, flat pasta sheets layered with fillings and baked until the cheese melts. The word "lasagna" may come from the Latin word lasanum, which means "cooking pot" or "chamber pot", or from the Greek flatbread laganon. The dish is named after its labor-intensive preparation method and is considered one of the oldest pasta dishes in Italian cuisine.

  • nutmeg
  • 300g fresh lasagne sheets
  • white sauce (find a recipe in the method, or use shop-bought)
  • 125g ball mozzarella, torn into thin strips

Description of Hamburger

A burger is a patty of ground beef grilled and placed between two halves of a bun. Slices of raw onion, lettuce, bacon, mayonnaise, and other ingredients add flavor. Burgers are considered an American food but are popular around the world. In Japan, teriyaki burgers are popular.

  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 500g good-quality beef mince
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 4 burger buns
  • All or any of the following to serve: sliced tomato, beetroot, horseradish sauce, mayonnaise, ketchup, handful iceberg lettuce, rocket, watercress

Description of Burrito

burrito, a common cylindrical food item of Mexican and American origin consisting of a tortilla wrapped around a mixed filling of such ingredients as meat, cheese, beans, and vegetables. The burrito differs from the taco in that the circular tortilla in the latter case is simply folded, not wrapped.
